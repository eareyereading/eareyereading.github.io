An Intelligent Reading App Designed for English Learning

Provides comprehensive reading enhancement features, equipped with real-human narration and highlighted follow-along reading. It can convert any text into an audiobook, allowing you to efficiently practice listening anytime, anywhere.

Download Documentation

Key Features

We offer the following unique reading features.

Word Freq Analysis

Analyzes the frequency of words in a book, helping you quickly identify high-frequency vocabulary and improve reading comprehension and language learning efficiency.

Word Wise

Automatically displays dictionary definitions below difficult words, eliminating the need for manual lookup and ensuring smooth reading.

Bionic Reading

Boldens parts of words to guide the reader’s eyes, enabling faster information processing and improved reading focus.

Speed Reading

Displays book content word by word or sentence by sentence at high speed to train users' eyes and brain for faster text processing.

Cloze Deletion

Allows users to hide words in a book and replace them with underlines, perfect for English “fill-in-the-blank” exercises.

Blur Text

Blurs book text to assist with sentence-by-sentence intensive listening and retelling exercises, effectively improving English listening and speaking skills.
