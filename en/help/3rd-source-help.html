Third-Party Book Sources

TingYue currently includes four built-in third-party book sources. Follow the instructions below to download and read books. If you have other great third-party sources to recommend, please contact our WeChat support eareyereading to suggest them.

  • Project Gutenberg
  • Zlibrary
  • Magazine Reader
  • Magazine Lib

All of these sources are hosted on overseas websites, so access speed may be slow in mainland China.

If the site cannot be opened or downloads fail, try switching networks or using a VPN.
Search results in the TingYue bookstore already include books from these sources—no need to visit the sites manually.

Project Gutenberg

Project Gutenberg is the world’s first free eBook platform, offering over 60,000 public domain titles.

To download books:

  1. Tap “Project Gutenberg” to open the site.
  2. Search or browse for the book you wish to read.
  3. On the book detail page, tap “EPUB (with images)” or “EPUB (no images)” to download.

Zlibrary

Zlibrary is one of the largest free digital libraries in the world, with over 4.9 million eBooks available.

To download books:

  1. Tap “Zlibrary” to open the site.
  2. Search or browse for the book you wish to read.
  3. On the book detail page, tap “Download (epub/mobi/pdf/txt/rtf, xx MB)” to download.

Unregistered users can download up to 5 books per IP every 24 hours. After registration, the daily limit increases to 10.

Magazine Reader

Magazine Reader offers a wide variety of English magazines covering news, culture, technology, fashion, and more.

Tap “Magazine Reader” to visit the site and explore or download the magazines you’re interested in.

Magazine Lib

Magazine Lib features a large collection of high-quality English magazines—an excellent resource for improving reading and listening skills.

Tap “Magazine Lib” to open the site, browse, and download the magazines you want to read.
