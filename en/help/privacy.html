Privacy Policy

Protecting customer privacy and your information security is TingYue’s primary responsibility. We strive to collect only data necessary to improve your experience and believe it is essential to inform you about what we collect and why before collection, enabling you to make informed choices.

USER CONSENT

By downloading, installing, and using the TingYue mobile application, you voluntarily provide written consent for the processing of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

GENERAL TERMS

1.1. This Privacy Policy constitutes an integral part of the End User License Agreement (EULA) for the TingYue mobile application. Your adoption of the application implies full acceptance of these terms.

1.2. The methods for processing Personal Information include actions such as collection, recording, systematization, storage, updating, retrieval, use, transfer, depersonalization, blocking, deletion, and destruction, whether automated or non-automated.

1.3. Terms from the EULA and other agreements with the User remain applicable unless explicitly stated otherwise in this Policy.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

2.1. When using the TingYue application in regular mode, users do not actively provide personally identifiable information. The application is designed to avoid creating identifiable records during normal usage.

2.2. Personal Information may include:

  • Your email address or social media account information provided during product feedback surveys (for users who have completed purchases)
  • User alias, display name, profile photo, and biography if you choose to create a personal profile
  • Information about books you play or read, including your account details, device information, IP address, reading position within the application, time and duration of reading and playback sessions

2.3. The User acknowledges the use of third-party software components within the application, which may automatically collect and transmit depersonalized data to their respective servers.

THIRD-PARTY SOFTWARE

Third-party software components integrated into TingYue may include:

  • Firebase Analytics: For application usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • AnalyticsKit: Internal analytics framework for tracking user engagement and feature usage

These components may collect device information, query data, and other details of user activity. All collected data undergoes depersonalization processing. Data collection by third-party components is governed by their respective privacy policies, and TingYue does not assume direct responsibility for their data handling practices.

CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

TingYue does not intentionally collect personal data from children under 16 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently retained personal information from a child under 16, we will promptly erase such records. Parents or guardians who discover that their child has provided personal information to TingYue should contact us immediately.

PURPOSES OF PERSONAL INFORMATION PROCESSING

TingYue may use Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • Communicating with users regarding product feedback, support requests, and service updates
  • Marketing activities, including notifications about new books and features available on TingYue Bookstore
  • Statistical research and analysis to improve application functionality
  • Personalizing your TingYue experience based on reading history and preferences
  • Targeted advertising using anonymized metrics

USER’S RIGHTS

Users have the following rights regarding their Personal Information:

  • Right to be informed about data collection and processing activities
  • Right to access their personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to restrict processing of their data
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to request deletion of their personal data
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, users should contact TingYue directly through the official website or support channels.

DATA RETENTION

Personal Information will be stored for as long as necessary or permitted by applicable law. TingYue periodically reviews data processing systems to ensure data relevance and compliance with retention requirements.

DATA PROTECTION

TingYue stores and protects Personal Information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction by implementing appropriate technical and organizational security measures in accordance with internal security protocols.

TRANSFER OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

TingYue may transfer Personal Information to third parties under the following circumstances:

  • With explicit user consent
  • When necessary to maintain basic application functionality
  • To external service providers operating under data processing agreements
  • In connection with corporate asset transitions, mergers, or acquisitions

When sharing content from TingYue to other websites or social networks, any shared content is subject to the privacy policies of those respective services.

PROFILE AND SHARING FEATURES

If you wish to contact other TingYue users or share content with them, you may create a personal profile by providing a user alias and display name. You may optionally provide a profile photo, biography, and other information. TingYue stores this information under your account to enable access from any of your devices.

Your user handle, display name, and profile photo may appear alongside any reviews you publish and activities you share on TingYue. Other TingYue users may be able to find your TingYue profile through information you provide. You may update or remove information in your profile at any time.

When sharing online, carefully consider what you are making public. Each time you share, you should thoughtfully evaluate the content you are disclosing.

AUDIOBOOK FEATURE

The audiobook listening feature is a benefit of TingYue membership, allowing you to listen to original narrations of all books in the bookstore from any of your devices.

AMENDMENTS AND DELETION REQUESTS

For questions, amendments, or deletion requests regarding Personal Information, users can contact TingYue through:

  • Official website: https://www.eareyereading.com
  • Email: eareyereading@gmail.com

AMENDMENTS TO PRIVACY POLICY

TingYue reserves the right to unilaterally amend or terminate this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Users are advised to regularly check the current version of this Policy on the official website.

NON-PERSONAL INFORMATION

We may collect, use, transfer, or disclose non-personal information for any purpose. For example, we may aggregate non-personal information with data from other TingYue users to improve our services.

All information described above is collected and used in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
