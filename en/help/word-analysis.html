Word Frequency Analysis

  • How to Enable
  • Feature Overview
  • Word Classification

Ever wondered which words appear most frequently in a book? Which ones are key terms? Our word frequency analysis feature helps you quickly identify the core vocabulary, enhancing reading comprehension and language learning efficiency.

How to Enable

To analyze a book’s vocabulary, follow these steps:

  1. Open the e-book you want to analyze.
  2. Tap the icon in the navigation bar. If this icon is not visible, tap the More button on the right. Choose Book Word Freq to analyze the entire book or Chapter Word Freq for the current chapter.

Feature Overview

The word frequency analysis categorizes words into five groups based on the following workflow. It calculates the number of words in each category and their proportion of the total vocabulary.

flowchart TD
    A[Start] --> B{Found in Dictionary?}
    B --> |Yes| C{In Ignore List?}
    B --> |No| D[Unrecognized]
    C --> |Yes| E[Ignored]
    C --> |No| F{Marked as Known?}
    F --> |Yes| G[Known]
    F --> |No| H{Already Added to Learning?}
    H --> |Yes| I[Learning]
    H --> |No| J[To Learn]

Additionally, the system tracks words included in your vocabulary list and calculates their proportion, providing insight into vocabulary coverage.

Word Classification

  • To Learn
    Words found in the dictionary but not yet marked. In this list:

    • Tap to mark a word as Known.
    • Tap to add it to Learning.
    • Displays word frequency. Tap the number to view sentences containing the word.
    • Words are ranked by frequency, with the most frequent at the top.
    • Sort by Frequency, Word, Ranking, Length, or Position for easy management.

  • Learning
    Words added to the learning list.

  • Known
    Words marked as known.

  • Ignored
    Words in the Ignore List.

  • Unrecognized
    Words not found in the dictionary.
